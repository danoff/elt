Project

Chicago Cultural Research Poster Project

This project will have students find an aspect of Chicago culture to study. Ideas could be sports, music, fashion, hotels, colleges, etc.

Project Goals

  • Develop a clear and easily understandable research essay
  • Develop a beautiful and informative poster
  • Deliver a crisp 2 to 3 minute presentation

Requirements

Your Essay must:
  • Be at least 800 words
  • Be on pages with 1.5 inch margins all around
  • Be hand-written and original; do not use somebody else's essay
  • Have a easily understood and clean table of results
  • Have 5 labled sections: Introduction, Research, Results, Analysis and Conclusion
Your Questionnaire must:
  • Have 10 Questions
  • Ask for the respondant's AGE, GENDER and NATIONALITY
  • Be given to at least 25 people
Your Poster must:
  • Have at least 4 images all of which must be either in the Public Domain or Copyright Licensed
  • Have summaries of your introduction, research, survey results, analysis and conclusion
Your Presentation must:
  • Be 2 to 3 minutes
  • Be delivered without notes
