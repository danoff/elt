The College of Chicago's Intensive English Program 200 (COC IEP 200)

An Intensive English Program course for English as a Second Language adult students at the College of Chicago (room 104) meeting Monday through Thursday from 9:30am to 3:00pm. This calendar is for 2013 Spring Trimester Block 1 from April 22nd to May 16th 2013.

      • ISBN: 978-1-4130-0736-7. Buy on Amazon. Find at your library via Wikipedia's Book Sources.
    • Haugnes, N. & Maher, B. (2009). NorthStar: Reading and writing level 2 (3rd ed.) Student Book. F. Boyd & C. Numrich (Eds.) White Plains, NY: Pearson Education.
      • ISBN: 978-0-13-240991-9. Publisher's Book Website. Buy on Amazon. Find at your library via Wikipedia's Book Sources.
    • Moses, A. (2002). Frozen pizza and other slices of life. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.
      • ISBN: 978-0-521-75078-3. Publisher's Book Website. Buy on Amazon. Find at your library via Wikipedia's Book Sources.
    • News for You ISSN: 0884-3910.
    • Plus other electronic books, articles, and materials linked to from the course calendar

    • Firefox installed on student's laptop or a USB stick
